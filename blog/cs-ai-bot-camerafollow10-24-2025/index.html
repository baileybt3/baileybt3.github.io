Camera Follow Feature

Added a feature for the camera to follow the bomb carrier.

Camera Following Bomb Carrier


I wanted to try and improve the camera's functionality somehow. I first thought about just adding a bunch of camera to get all of the angles, but that felt very tedious and I wanted to make the stream feel more dynamic. I decided to add functionality to the cameras so they followed the bomb carrier. In my first pass, the bomb followed the carrier well and looked much more enjoyable to watch.
I ran into an issue of the camera switching to a camera that couldn't actually see the bomb carrier. This is because I was just checking the distance between the bomb and each camera. This didn't work well because occasionally the camera would be closer through a wall so you couldn't even see what was happening. This was fixed by adding a check to see if the bomb carrier is actually viewable. This turned out pretty good and I believe it is much more enjoyable to watch.
