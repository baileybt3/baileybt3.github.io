Attempt at Modeling a Character

In an attempt to keep my capstone project unique, I tried to model a character.

The Process

When I first started modeling the character it went pretty well. I was aiming for a blocky, Roblox-like style. The modeling itself wasn’t too bad and I ended up with a decent low-poly base. I started losing patience (and confidence 😅) when it came time to create seams and texture the model. It felt like I was burning time when I knew there were better assets available than what I could produce. This is about as far as I got:

Because I’m on a time crunch I decided to look for free asset packs. I came across the Kenney Blocky Characters and they felt perfect for getting the base game complete.

These models are amazing for free, commercially usable assets, and they include a bunch of ready-to-use animations. I’ve already got a couple into the game and started on animation:
